Shake the Hand

In this exercise, you will be able to practice the appropriate way to greet someone with a handshake. This will challenge you to practice shaking a hand online to prepare you for real word hand shaking. Drag to let the hands meet for a handshake, hold it for the appropriate amount of time and click to let go. Beware to not hold on for too long, there is nothing worse than that uncomfortably long hand contact.
